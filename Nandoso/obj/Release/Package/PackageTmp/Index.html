  • What is flame-grilled chicken ?

    Flame-grilled chicken is chicken prepared as food by grilling whether in a home kitchen, over a fire, or with a professional rotisserie (rotary spit). Generally, the chicken is grilled with its own fat and juices by circulating the meat during grilling, and therefore, are usually cooked exposed to fire or heat with some type of rotary grill so that the circulation of these fats and juices is as efficient as possible. Flame-grilled chicken is a dish that appears in a wide variety of cuisines worldwide.
